Over the past two decades staff and students made several attempts to set up the Computing Alumni of the University of Colombo but had very little success. Twenty-fifth anniversary activities of the UCSC brought the Alumni together and an interim committee of the Alumni Association of UCSC was formed on 9th July 2010. Through the alumni, our graduates could closely follow the Sri Lanka’s premier computing institute’s progress, activities and initiatives while keeping in touch with colleagues and advances in the field. Alumni could also contribute towards institutional development through participation, contributions, establishing new links with the industry and helping & guiding the students.
