Message of the Principal

It is privilege to be able to serve a college with a remarkable history in the city of Matara comprising over 3500 school student population. We strive to give the best for the students with a dedicated staff for the needs and requirements of the students.

The internet is used in communication in every field through out the world. Most of the counties get the use of internet in every field for communication. But in Sri Lanka, it is known that our usage of internet is in a low level. But it has spread out in every field. Because of the internet the qualitative development of education has increased. We are happy to be in the forefront in the field of sports,IT and with our academic excellence.

OUR VISION
Gifting of an excellent Thomian to the nation and the universe.
OUR MISSION
Creating a perfect individual, well-equipped with knowledge, attitudes and skills, who can face the challenging situations with confidence.
Objectives of St. Thomas College, Matara.
  1. Providing equal educational opportunities for all students.
  2. Providing encouragement and secure locality for all students through implementation of well-organised educational programme.
  3. Building up of self confidence in all students in order to promote their achievement levels by developing their potential.
  4. Developing and implementing a conducive family environment within the school premises.
